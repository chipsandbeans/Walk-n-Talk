Fairfield Walkers


The Fairfield Walkers group meets every evening from Monday to Friday at 5 PM at the entrance to the Fairfield.

Participants can enjoy a stroll at a pace that suits them, as there are both fast and slow walkers in attendance.

This inclusive environment encourages everyone to join and enjoy the benefits of walking together.

Whether you are looking to socialize or simply enjoy the outdoors, the Fairfield Walkers provide a welcoming community for all.

Lee Fields Walking Club


Every Monday, Tuesday and Friday at 6pm the Lee Fields Walking Club meets in the Lee Fields Car Park before embarking on our walk.

This route is accessible to those who are wheelchair bound or differently abled individuals who struggle with mobility.

All levels of experience and age groups welcome!
