Disability Friendly



We here at Walk 'n' Talk make every effort to be as inclusive as possible for those of us who are wheelchair bound or have other mobility issues. We make sure that our walking routes follow well-paved paths which have adequate spacing for wheelchair users. All of our walking routes are on flat surfaces, we aim to avoid hills as they make the route unnecessarily difficult for wheelchair users and those of us who use walking aids such as zimmer frames and walking sticks.
